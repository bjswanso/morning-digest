Personal Care

**1. 🧠 Mental Health — Defrag Your Brain (10 min)**

Do a "brain dump" before your day locks in. Set a 10-minute timer and write, without editing, every task, worry, and open loop currently occupying RAM. Then sort the list into three columns: *Act today*, *Schedule later*, *Delete.* The INTJ cortex runs cleaner when it isn't also serving as backup storage. Research consistently links expressive writing and structured cognitive offloading to reduced stress and improved decision quality.

**2. 💪 Physical Health — Micro-Mobility Break (5 min)**

Set a recurring alarm for a 5-minute movement break — not a walk, not a workout, just 5 minutes of deliberate stretching or bodyweight movement (shoulder rolls, hip flexor stretch, 10 squats). Prolonged sitting compresses spinal discs and dampens alertness; even a single brief break measurably improves circulation and cognitive performance for the hour that follows. Think of it as rebooting a process that's been running too long.

**3. 🤝 Strengthening Your Relationship — The 2-Minute Appreciation Ritual (2 min)**

Before you and your spouse go your separate ways this morning, name one specific thing you genuinely appreciated about them *this week* — not a generic compliment, but a precise observation. ("I noticed you handled that logistics problem with the kids on Wednesday without asking me to context-switch — that mattered.") Specificity signals attention, and attention signals investment. Relationship researchers at the Gottman Institute identify expressed appreciation as one of the strongest predictors of long-term partnership satisfaction. Two minutes; no app required.
